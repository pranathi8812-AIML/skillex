How It Works

Four simple steps to start exchanging skills in your neighbourhood

1. Join

Register with your name, email, and neighbourhood ward. You get 10 free credits to start.

2. Post or Browse

Post a skill you can offer or browse requests from people in your area who need help.

3. Exchange

Agree to help someone and complete the exchange. Credits transfer automatically on completion.

4. Build Trust

Rate each other after every exchange. Build your community reputation over time.
