👋 How to Use Skillex

1️⃣
Create your account

Click "Register" at the top. Enter your name, email, password and your area name.

2️⃣
You get 10 free credits

When you join, you automatically get 10 credits. Think of credits like tokens for exchanging help.

3️⃣
Post what you can do

Click "+ Post Skill" to tell people what you can help with — cooking, stitching, tutoring, repairs, anything!

4️⃣
Find help near you

Click "Skill Board" to see what others are offering. You can filter by your area or category.

5️⃣
Send an exchange request

Found something useful? Click on it and press "Send Exchange Request". The other person will help you.

6️⃣
Credits move automatically

Once the help is done, credits go from your account to the helper. No cash needed!

7️⃣
Rate each other

After the exchange, give stars to the person who helped you. This builds trust in the community.

💬
Send messages

Click "Message" on anyone's profile to chat. They need to accept your request first for privacy.
